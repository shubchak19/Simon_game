Instructions

  • Step 1:

    Press the Power button to power on the game.

  • Step 2:

    Press the Start button to start the game.

  • Step 3:

    The colors will flash in a particular order one by one and stop.
    You have to remember the order.

  • Step 4:

    Once they stop you are supposed to click on the colors one by one in the same order.

  • Step 5:

    If the order is correct you will step up to the next level.
    If not then you can try again.

  • Note:

    In Hard mode, on every failed attempt you will jump back to Level 0
